Delete Your Account

If you would like to permanently delete your Shakthi Sankhya account and associated data, please follow the steps below. Deleting your account will remove your profile, login credentials, stored mantra logs, and all personal data from our system.

  • Open the ShakthiSankhya App.
  • Open the burger menu and select on Settings.
  • Select Delete My Account and follow the steps.
Can't access your account on the app? Email support@shakthisankhya.org from your registered address, and we will process your deletion request.
For privacy and account-related queries, contact the Shakthi Sankhya support team anytime.
