Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shakthi Sankhya?

Shakthi Sankhya is a free, ad-free app designed for mantra counting, Japam tracking, spiritual progress visualization, and Gurukul-based community practice.

Who can use Shakthi Sankhya?

Both independent practitioners and Gurus or spiritual mentors can use the app, with dedicated features and dashboards for each role.

Is the app really free and ad-free?

Yes. Shakthi Sankhya is always free and contains no advertisements, respecting the focus and serenity of your spiritual path.

How does the Japam counter work?

The Instant Japam Counter lets you tap for each repetition, set target counts, and instantly see your progress—perfect for focused practice.

What’s included in session history?

Session history provides complete logs of personal or group practices, including dates, repetition counts, and milestone achievements for meaningful reflection.

How is my data kept secure?

All personal feedback and progress are encrypted and only accessible to authorized Gurus or admins, ensuring privacy for every user.

How can I give feedback or request new features?

Practitioners can suggest features, report bugs, or communicate with Gurus and the app community directly through in-app feedback forms.

Can Gurus organize and manage Gurukuls?

Yes. Gurus can create Gurukuls, assign goals, track the collective progress of disciples, and provide guidance and motivation using advanced oversight tools.

Does the app work on mobile and tablet?

Shakthi Sankhya is fully optimized for mobile and tablet devices with a responsive, accessible interface.

What makes Shakthi Sankhya unique?

It is specifically built for the Gurukul spiritual model, yet supports individual practice, with always-free access, next-level privacy, and zero distractions.
