Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 17, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Shakthi Sankhya (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Hindu spiritual application. We value your privacy and honor your spiritual data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum necessary information for you to use spiritual tracking features, such as user login, japam count, and app statistics. Prayer and mantra logs are kept private and secure.

How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your in-app experience (mantra counts, reminders).
  • For app improvements and analytics – all data is anonymized.
  • To provide support and respond to your queries.
  • To comply with legal requirements if necessary.

Spiritual Data Sensitivity

We do not commercialize your spiritual practices or profile you for advertising. Prayer, mantra, and spiritual logs are respected as personal data.

Children's Privacy

The app is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Parental consent is required for child accounts and usage.

Security Measures

  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely, following industry best practices.
  • Spiritual records may be stored locally or securely in the cloud, as per app feature needs.

Your Rights

You can request deletion or modification of your spiritual data at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Links and Resources

External resources or links provided via the app are not governed by this Privacy Policy. Please review the policies of those sites before use.

Changes to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as required by law or evolving app features. Please check this page periodically for updates.
